Ski Racers

Ski Racers Club is situated half way up the Rock Garden ski run, close to the main chairlift at Whakapapa. It is a friendly, family oriented club with excellent facilities. Being located up on the ski field, Ski Racers is ideally located for ski-in and ski-out. The club accommodates 32 people in bunk room sleeping arrangements, with shared facilities and common living areas. Ski Racers prides itself on having a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere. Many people have enjoyed what the Club has to offer. Discover more about joining the Club or staying with us.
