2018 Nightly Fees

Our fully refundable rates* (if cancelled more than 7 days prior to use) are as follows:

-Member Senior - $42

-Member Junior 11 to under 17 - $32

-Member Junior under 11 - $27

-Non-Member Senior - $75

-Non-Member Junior 11 to under 17 - $65

-Non-Member Junior under 11 - $50

-School Rate - $49

- Non-Member, mid-week group (>10 people) 20% discount

 *Rate is non transferable but is refundable in full, if cancelled more than 5 days prior to booking; no refund is given if cancellation is made 4 or less days from booking. No refund is given if you choose to leave early.

Non Member Booking Information


Members are welcome to bring Non Members with them and there is no waiting period to confirm bookings. We like to encourage members to bring friends and family to Ski Racers.
Members and their guests get priority, other Non Member bookings are confirmed two weeks prior.

Booking Rules

The booking system is a key part of how the Club functions. For it to work well, all bookings must go through the Booking Officer. It is not acceptable to turn up at the Club ‘unbooked’. The full rules are on the Booking Form.

Booking Forms



You’re one easy step away from a booking!

Click the link below to download a Booking Form.

BOOKING FORM 2017.docx


The form is a Word doc, just complete, “save as” the file with your name, ie: Booking Form - Jones family and email to Mike, our Booking Officer.

You must use a separate form for every booking. You can put all the guests for a booking on the same form, but different booking periods must each be on a different form.

Confirmation of your booking will be emailed back (or sent via text) within 24-48 hours. You are not booked in until your booking is confirmed. Payment must be made at time of booking, and your booking will only be confirmed once payment is made.

Any questions, email the Booking Officer at mikebroke@xtra.co.nz.

General Booking Information



Need to cancel? You must do so by emailing mikebroke@xtra.co.nz or text to 021 254 3101. A full refund is given if cancellation is made seven or more days out from booking, ie: Friday prior to Friday booking.

If you are interested in booking a school group, or taking a group from work or another club or society, please contact the Booking Officer via email at mikebroke@xtra.co.nz
